Muzlik davriga galaktik nazar: Yangi gipoteza

Muallif: Abdulloh Jalilov Gʻayrat oʻgʻli

Date of Publication: March 8, 2026

English Version 🇬🇧

Have you ever wondered if Earth's Ice Ages were truly isolated events? My hypothesis suggests a galactic cause. Our Solar System orbits the Galactic Center (Sagittarius A*) every 250 million years. As we drift through the galaxy's spiral arms, the change in cosmic radiation and thermal influence triggers simultaneous cooling across planets like Jupiter, Neptune, and even moons like Europa.

O‘zbekcha versiya 🇺🇿

Hech o‘ylab ko‘rganmisiz, muzlik davrlari aslida Yerning o‘ziga xos hodisasi emasdir? Men bu jarayon yanada kengroq, galaktik ko‘lamda sodir bo‘ladi deb hisoblayman. Quyosh sistemasi galaktika markazidagi Sagittarius A* atrofida aylanar ekan, bizning spiral armalardan (qirlardan) uzoqlashishimiz kosmik haroratni pasaytiradi. Bu esa nafaqat Yerni, balki Yupiter, Neptun va Yevropa kabi yo‘ldoshlarni ham muzlatib yuborishga qodir global jarayondir.
